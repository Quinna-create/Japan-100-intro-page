Unit 1: Big Pictures

In this unit, we will be looking at big pictures. What do we mean when we're talking about Japanese literature? With a long and rich tradition of texts to choose from, why are we looking at the texts that we are this semester? In short, what is the key concept grounding this course, and how are we going to understand more about it?

We'll also talk about some fundamental tools we use in understanding literature and really any kind of cultural artifact. This includes the material in this course, but also things you will encounter every day in your daily life.
